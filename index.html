Who We Are

We are not passive capital.

Lullwater Capital is a European angel investment group backing ambitious founders building scalable businesses with real traction. We invest $10,000–$75,000 in Seed and Series A companies across Madrid, Paris, and London.

"Beyond capital, we offer hands-on mentorship, strategic support, and access to our network across Europe. We move fast, think clearly, and back founders who are building something real."

Founded by Lukas Van de Vrede and Leon Gutierrez — IE University alumni united by ambition and a shared belief that the best investments are built on genuine partnership.

Selection Criteria

What we look for.

We invest in
—Founders with conviction, domain expertise, and a clear vision
—Proven revenue traction — the business model works
—Scalable unit economics with a path to strong margins
—Large addressable market — we back ambitious ideas
—Capital efficiency — teams that do more with less
—Openness to mentorship and strategic collaboration
We don't invest in
—Pre-revenue or pure idea-stage companies
—Businesses without a clear path to scalability
—Founders unwilling to take feedback or iterate
—Sectors with structural regulatory barriers to growth

How It Works

Our process.

01
Application
Submit your pitch deck and a brief summary of your traction and financials. We respond to every application within 5 business days.
02
First-Round Screening
Shortlisted companies present to our Deal Lead in a focused 30-minute session. We evaluate your financial model, unit economics, and growth strategy.
03
Due Diligence
We conduct financial due diligence, market analysis, competitive landscape review, and founder background checks. Honest and thorough.
04
Investor Presentation
Companies passing due diligence present to the full Lullwater Capital investor group. Each angel receives a detailed Deal Memo prepared by our Deal Lead.
05
Decision & Close
Angels decide independently. Term sheet and documentation prepared. Target close within 2–4 weeks of investor approval. We move fast.
06
Post-Investment Partnership
Monthly check-ins, strategic support, and network introductions begin immediately. We are active partners, not passive observers.
